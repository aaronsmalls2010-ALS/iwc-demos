Our Firm

Two decades of Lowcountry counsel

Eversole Law Firm was founded in 2004 by Alysoun M. Eversole, who learned the craft of South Carolina real estate law under the mentorship of the late Sherwood N. Fender, Esq. before opening her own practice on King Street.

That foundation — paired with nine years of courtroom experience early in her career — shapes how the firm works today: thorough, strategic, and genuinely invested in every client. We take the time to understand your situation, explain your options plainly, and find the path that actually gets you where you need to go.

We're proud to be part of this community, and we treat our neighbors the way we'd want to be treated — with patience, candor, and care.
