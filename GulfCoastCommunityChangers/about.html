Our Story

ABOUT US

Purpose

OUR MISSION

Gulf Coast Community Changers LLC was founded with a simple but powerful purpose: to give youth in the Gulf Coast region a positive path forward through athletics, teamwork, and community. We believe every child deserves the chance to compete, grow, and build lasting character — both on and off the field.

Background

OUR STORY

The Gulf Coast Chargers launched their inaugural season with a vision: to create a youth organization that goes beyond sports. Now entering our 2nd annual season in 2026, we offer football, cheerleading, and dance programs for children ages 5 through 12 in the Gulfport, Mississippi area.

What started as a grassroots effort by Coach PJ and a team of dedicated volunteers has grown into a community staple. Our registration camps, held at James Hill Park and on 33rd Avenue in Gulfport, bring families together every summer to prepare for a season of competition, learning, and fun.
