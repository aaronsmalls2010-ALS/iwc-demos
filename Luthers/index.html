Our Story

A Beaufort
Waterfront Institution

Housed in the historic Luther's Pharmacy building on Bay Street, Luther's Rare & Well Done has become the heart of Beaufort's waterfront scene. From locals grabbing lunch to visitors winding down after a day on the water, everyone finds their spot here.

Great food, cold drinks, live music, and views that never get old. That's the Luther's way.
